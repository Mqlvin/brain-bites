Welcome to Brain Bites


What is brain bites?


Brain bites is a project designed to take long videos, and use the power of AI to create condensed, chaptered videos.


How do I use brain bites?


To explore different videos, use the navigation bar on the left, and click 'browse'. Find a topic to watch and enjoy!


Who made brain bites?


Brain bites is a project for the Inversity team challenge on improving education. It was made by Henry, Josh and Zaki.
